Janet's Bio

Hi my name is Anyijukire Janet, currently a student at AkiraChix. Janet was born in 2001, March 3rd in Kanungu District the South Western part of Uganda to Byaruhanga Frank and Niwamanya Ruth as their first daughter.

Janet went to Kihihi Progressive school for her primary school, Immaculate heart Girls school Nyakibale for her ordinary level and San Giovanni school Makiro for her Advanced level(high school).

She has been a leader serving in her former schools in different roles;
In Primary she was the chapel prefect from 2012-2013.
In Ordinary level she was the leader of Girl Guides. She was rewarded with a certificate.
In advanced level she was the school speaker. She was rewarded with a certificate of merit.
Currently she is the entertainment representative at AkiraChix.
